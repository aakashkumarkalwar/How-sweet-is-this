Royce’ was founded in 1983 in Sapporo, the capital of Hokkaido, Japan’s Northernmost island. Quality, originality and surprise have been at the core of what we do, since our inception. We pride ourselves in making unique chocolate using some of the finest ingredients sourced from across globe. Mindfully crafted and meticulously packaged, we pay close attention to every detail that goes into the making of our indulgent chocolate.
